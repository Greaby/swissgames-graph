SwissGames Map

Interactive map of the game related activities in Switzerland.


Last data update: 24.05.2020


SwissGamesMap maps the relationships between the different people related to the game industry in Switzerland.
The data is aggregated from the twitter accounts @gameculture_ch, @SGDAch and @igdach.
